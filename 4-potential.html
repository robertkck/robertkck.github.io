Chapter 4 Potential

In this chapter, we want to determine which countries might have some of the prerequisites for developing an advantage in the four technologies of interest. We build on the fact that countries fnd it easier to innovate in technologies that are related to technologies they are already good at4. As a measure of technology proximity, we use the relative frequency with which two technology classes (in fact technology codes) appear as part of the same patent. We then analyse which countries are good at patenting the technologies we identifed as being ‘nearby’ our four low-carbon technologies.
